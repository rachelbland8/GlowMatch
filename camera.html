Photo Upload / Camera Step

Take or upload a photo in even lighting so the app can estimate general tone and skin traits. This is only a cosmetic estimate, not a medical diagnosis.

Tips

For the best estimate:

• Use bright, even lighting

• Face the camera directly

• Avoid heavy shadows

• Avoid filters if possible

• Minimal makeup gives more accurate skin analysis
